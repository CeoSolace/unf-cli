# Privacy Policy

UnfilteredUK values your privacy and strives to collect as little personal information as possible. This document outlines what data we collect and how it is used.

## Data Collection

- **Account Information**: When you register, we collect your username, email address and a cryptographic public key. Your private key is stored only on your device.
- **Metadata**: We store metadata such as server memberships and timestamps. Message content is encrypted on your device and cannot be decrypted by us.
- **Logs**: We log IP addresses and user agent strings for security and abuse prevention. These logs are retained for a limited time.

## Data Use

- We use your email address to send account recovery information and important notifications.
- Aggregated, anonymised data may be used to improve the service.
- We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## Cookies

We use cookies for authentication and session management. Cookies are never used for tracking or advertising purposes.
